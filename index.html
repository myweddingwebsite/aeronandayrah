Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

We recommend arriving at Our Lady of Guadalupe Parish by 1:00 PM to ensure you are seated before the ceremony begins at 2:00 PM. For the reception at Caliraya Heights, doors open at 5:00 PM.

Due to limited seating, we kindly ask that you attend as per the names indicated on your invitation. If you have questions about your invitation, please reach out to us directly.

Parking is available at both venues. Our Lady of Guadalupe Parish has designated parking near the church grounds, and Caliraya Heights Resort provides ample parking space for guests.

Caliraya Heights Resort and Events Place offers accommodations on-site. There are also several hotels and resorts in the Laguna area for guests who need overnight stays. We recommend booking in advance.

Please dress in semi-formal attire in light pastel or neutral colors. Denim and overly casual wear are kindly discouraged. Check our Dress Code section for detailed guidelines for each role and gender.
